üî¨ 3D Reconstruction: Why It Matters

Current Limitations (2D Video)

  • Limited spatial information
  • Difficult to measure dimensions
  • Can't revisit from different angles
  • Poor integration with GIS/CAD

3D Reconstruction Benefits

  • Digital twins of pipe interiors
  • Quantitative measurements (crack width, offsets)
  • Multi-viewpoint analysis in post-processing
  • GIS integration for asset management

Data Fusion: Combines 2D imagery + kinematic data (IMU) + range measurements (LiDAR/laser profiler) ‚Üí Comprehensive 3D model with visual texture

üîÑ Proposed Integrated Workflow

Seamless Integration with Current Practice

  1. Field Inspection: Crawler captures HD video + laser profiler + IMU data (no change to operator workflow)
  2. Overnight Processing: Automated pipeline runs 3D reconstruction + AI damage detection
  3. Next Morning: Certified operator opens WinCan VX with pre-flagged defects and 3D model
  4. Human Review: Operator validates AI detections, reviews 3D reconstruction, finalizes NASSCO coding
  5. Export: Data flows to VueWorks (asset management) and ArcGIS (GIS)

Key Principle: Human-in-loop maintained for quality assurance. AI accelerates, doesn't replace, expert review.
